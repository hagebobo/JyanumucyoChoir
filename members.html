Our Members

A Family of Voices United in Faith

JYANUMUCYO is blessed with 60 dedicated active members who come together regularly to practice, perform, and grow in their musical and spiritual journey. Additionally, we have 60 members who are working in various places across Rwanda and beyond, maintaining their connection to our choir family.

We pride ourselves on our inclusive community where every member is valued and encouraged to develop their talents. Through regular rehearsals, workshops, and performances, our members continuously refine their skills while building lasting friendships.

Ordained Pastors

🎶 Soprano Section

Our soprano section brings the highest voices to our harmonies, carrying melodies with clarity and beauty. They provide the brightness and brilliance that crowns our choral sound.

🎵 Alto Section

The alto section provides rich, warm tones that complement and support the entire choir. Their harmonies add depth and character to every piece we perform.

🎼 Tenor Section

Our tenor voices bring strength and vitality to the upper male register. They bridge the gap between the higher and lower voices, creating seamless harmonies.

🎹 Bass Section

The bass section provides the foundational support for our entire sound. Their deep, resonant voices anchor our harmonies and give our music its power and presence.
