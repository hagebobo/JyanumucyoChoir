Our History

30 Years of Faith, Music, and Community

1995

The Beginning

JYANUMUCYO Choir was founded at EAR Diocese Butare with a vision to create a musical ministry that would glorify God and serve the community. A small group of dedicated individuals came together, united by their love for worship music.

1990s

Growth & Development

Through the challenging times, JYANUMUCYO remained a source of hope and unity. The choir grew in numbers and reputation, becoming known for their powerful harmonies and spiritual depth. Regular performances at the cathedral drew larger audiences.

2000s

Expansion & Recognition

JYANUMUCYO expanded its repertoire and began participating in regional festivals. Our first members began entering pastoral ministry, demonstrating the choir's impact beyond music. We became recognized as one of the leading church choirs in the region.

2010s

Spiritual Formation

The choir strengthened its focus on spiritual formation, establishing mentorship programs and leadership development initiatives. More members entered pastoral ministry, bringing our total to thirteen ordained pastors - a testament to our spiritual impact.

2020s

Modern Era

JYANUMUCYO adapted to modern challenges while maintaining its core values. We expanded our outreach through digital platforms and continued to attract new members. Today, with 60 active members and 60 alumni, we remain committed to our mission.

2026

Looking Forward

As we celebrate over 30 years of ministry, JYANUMUCYO continues to thrive. We remain dedicated to musical excellence, spiritual formation, and community building, looking forward to many more years of service and worship.
