Our History

30 Years of Faith, Music, and Community

1995

The Beginning

JYANUMUCYO Choir was founded at EAR Diocese Butare with a vision to create a musical ministry that would glorify God and serve the community.

1990s

Growth & Development

Through the challenging times, JYANUMUCYO remained a source of hope and unity.

2000s

Expansion & Recognition

JYANUMUCYO expanded its repertoire and began participating in regional festivals.

2010s

Spiritual Formation

The choir strengthened its focus on spiritual formation, establishing mentorship programs.

2020s

Modern Era

JYANUMUCYO adapted to modern challenges while maintaining its core values.

2026

Looking Forward

As we celebrate over 30 years of ministry, JYANUMUCYO continues to thrive.
