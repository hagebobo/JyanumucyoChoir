Why Join JYANUMUCYO?

JYANUMUCYO offers more than just musical training. We provide a supportive community where you can grow spiritually, develop your talents, and make lifelong friendships. Whether you're an experienced singer or just beginning, there's a place for you here.

Our choir has a rich 41-year legacy of excellence, and we're always looking for passionate individuals to join our family.
