Our Pastors

Ordained ministers who were raised and formed through JYANUMUCYO Choir — a living testimony of our spiritual mission

From Choir to Pulpit

One of the greatest honours of JYANUMUCYO Choir is not only the music we sing, but the lives we shape. Since our founding in 1995, the Holy Spirit has called many of our members into full pastoral ministry. Today we celebrate

13 Ordained Pastors

who were nurtured, discipled, and spiritually formed within our choir family — now serving God's people across Rwanda and beyond.
