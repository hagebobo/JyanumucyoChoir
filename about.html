About JYANUMUCYO

A Legacy of Faith, Excellence, and Community

Our Story

JYANUMUCYO Choir was founded in 1995 at EAR Diocese Butare with a vision to create a musical ministry that would glorify God and uplift the community.

Our name, JYANUMUCYO, reflects our commitment to bringing light and joy through music.

Throughout our journey, we have touched countless hearts, nurtured spiritual growth, and created a family bound by faith and music.
