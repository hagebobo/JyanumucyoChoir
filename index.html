Welcome to JYANUMUCYO Choir

For over three decades, JYANUMUCYO has been a beacon of spiritual inspiration and musical excellence in Rwanda. Founded in 1995 at EAR Diocese Butare, our choir has touched countless hearts through powerful worship and harmonious melodies.

We are more than just a choir – we are a family united in faith, dedicated to glorifying God through music and nurturing the spiritual growth of our members.

Whether you're seeking spiritual enrichment, musical excellence, or a welcoming community, JYANUMUCYO invites you to join us in our mission to spread joy and faith through song.
