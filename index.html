About Us

Welcome to JYANUMUCYO Choir

JYANUMUCYO is a vibrant and dynamic choir from the EAR (Episcopal Anglican Church of Rwanda) Diocese of Butare, dedicated to spreading joy, faith, and unity through music since 1995. As part of the NNCA (National Network of Catholic Artists) section, we have been touching hearts and souls for 29 years.

Based in Butare, our choir brings together talented voices from diverse backgrounds, united by our passion for worship and excellence in choral performance. We currently have 60 active members, with an additional 60 members working in various places around the country, maintaining their connection to our choir family.

With a rich repertoire spanning traditional hymns to contemporary gospel, JYANUMUCYO continues to be a beacon of musical excellence and spiritual devotion in the EAR Diocese Butare. We are particularly proud that our choir has been a spiritual foundation for 7 ordained pastors who have gone on to serve in ministry, a testament to our commitment to both musical and spiritual formation.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To glorify God through exceptional musical worship and to spread the message of faith, hope, and love to our community and beyond. We strive to create transformative worship experiences that bring people closer to God through the power of harmonious voices.

Our Vision

To be a leading voice in spiritual music, inspiring generations through our dedication to artistic excellence and spiritual depth. We envision a community united through song, where every voice matters and every performance reflects God's glory.

Our Values

Excellence in worship, Unity in diversity, Spiritual growth, Community service, Musical innovation, Respect and dignity, Continuous learning, Faith-centered performance, Inclusive fellowship, and Joyful celebration of God's gifts.

Our Members

A Family of Voices

JYANUMUCYO is blessed with 60 dedicated active members who come together regularly to practice, perform, and grow in their musical and spiritual journey. Additionally, we have 60 members who are working in various places across Rwanda and beyond, maintaining their connection to our choir family.

We pride ourselves on our inclusive community where every member is valued and encouraged to develop their talents. Through regular rehearsals, workshops, and performances, our members continuously refine their skills while building lasting friendships.

Each member plays a vital role in our success, from sopranos and altos to tenors and basses, creating the rich harmonies that JYANUMUCYO is known for. We are also proud that our choir has nurtured 7 individuals who went on to become ordained pastors, demonstrating our deep commitment to spiritual formation alongside musical excellence.
